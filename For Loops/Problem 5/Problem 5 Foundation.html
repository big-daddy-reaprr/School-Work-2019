    • This is a money clicker game. The objective of the game is to acquire as much money as you can.

    MPS = Money per second
    Money = Total amount

    Sell:
    For each tier of sellable goods, you must sell the previous goods a total of 25 times. This increases Money
    E.G. to sell Home-Made Brownies, you must sell 25 Lemonade.

    Shop:
    Items in the shop increase your MPS. Buy items with money, the higher MPS, the more money you make per second.
    There is no limit on the ammount of items you can buy or need to buy to be eligible to buy the next tier.
